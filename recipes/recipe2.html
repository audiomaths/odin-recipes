Cream Peas

  1. Combine Peas, Water, and salt in medium saucepan. Bring to a boil, then stir in butter
  2. Whisk together cream, flour, and sugar in a small bowl
  3. Stir mixture into peas
  4. Cook over medium-high heat until thick and bubbly
  5. Roughly cook for 5 minutes!
